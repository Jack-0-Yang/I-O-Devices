Scanner

2D Scanner

Input hard-copy documents and convert into electronic forms to be stored in computers.

The cover is raised
-the document is placed on a glass panel
-the cover is closed.

↓

A bright light illuminates the document.

↓

A scan head moves across the document and scans the whole page.It produces an image of the document which is sent to a lens through a series of mirrors. Then the lens focuses the image.

↓

The focused image falls onto a CCD and is turned into an electric form. Then, software produces a digital image.

Computers with OCR can convert text in the document into a text file format, so the image can be edited by a word processor.
If the document is an image, then it is converted into an image file, such as JPEG.
